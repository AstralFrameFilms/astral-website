The Psychic Hour — Feature Film · Non-Union Submission deadline: May 26 Auditions: May 29, 7 PM · May 31, 2 PM
Kaia Femme-identifying · 18+ to play late teens · Open ethnicity

Kaia leads with confidence and humor, the kind of person who talks her way into anywhere and makes it look effortless. She's spontaneous, someone who loves without hesitation. Kaia is Perry's prom date. Their relationship is new, electric, and the emotional center of the film's opening.

This role requires a chase sequence and horror-style choreography.
Perry Femme-identifying · 18+ to play late teens · Open ethnicity

Perry is deliberate and observant, someone who notices everything and says less than she thinks. She has been quietly in love with Kaia for longer than she'll admit. Their relationship becomes key to awakening her truest self.

Ellen Voice role · 18+

Ellen is a senior, a little shallow but sharp and self-aware enough to know something is wrong with her best friend but not quite brave enough to confront it. She calls a psychic show on prom night, which tells you everything about where she's at.

This is a voice-only role, recorded remotely or in studio.
